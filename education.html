Education

Learn how to identify deepfakes and protect yourself.

🧠 Visual cues

Look for the tell-tale signs

  • Inconsistent lighting and shadows across frames.
  • Blurry edges around hair, ears, and glasses.
  • Unnatural blinking patterns or lip movement.
  • Compression artifacts clustered near the face.

🎧 Audio cues

Listen closely

  • Audio-visual desynchronization.
  • Robotic cadence and unusual intonation.
  • Background noise that cuts unnaturally between frames.

🛡 Best practices

Stay safe online

  • Verify source accounts and cross-check with trusted outlets.
  • Treat sensational content with skepticism until verified.
  • Preserve originals; use watermarks where appropriate.
  • Report harmful deepfakes to platforms and authorities.
